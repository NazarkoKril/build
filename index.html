About us

Renoverend Contractor was founded 18 years ago by Robert Siutkowski. Over time, our construction company in the Randstad region has grown into a versatile building specialist in the field of renovation and remodeling. We primarily employ skilled Polish craftsmen, which allows us to offer a highly competitive and attractive hourly rate starting from €29.50. All of our skilled professionals guarantee quality, good service, and speed. Renoverend has a complete team of specialists on board, including carpenters, painters, plasterers, installers, and plumbers. This enables us to undertake a wide range of projects, from small to large, with expertise and proficiency.

Renoverend has been recognized for years as a reliable and professional construction partner by numerous reputable clients throughout the Randstad region and beyond. Our skilled employees are committed to maintaining this reputation by making every effort to complete each task in the future as well.
